Procare Photo Downloader

How to get your bearer token:
  1. Go to schools.procareconnect.com/dashboard and log in.
  2. Open DevTools (F12 or Cmd+Option+I) and click the Network tab.
  3. Reload the page, then filter by daily_activities in the request list.
  4. Click that request → Headers → find Authorization and copy the full value (starts with Bearer online_auth_...).
